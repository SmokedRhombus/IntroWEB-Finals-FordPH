Performance

The Ford F-150 is a formidable force on the road, delivering exceptional performance that is sure to impress. Its powerful engines, combined with its robust frame, enable it to tow heavy loads with ease and carry substantial payloads effortlessly. Whether you're hauling equipment for work or embarking on a weekend adventure, the F-150's performance capabilities are unmatched. Its off-road prowess is equally impressive, thanks to its available four-wheel drive and terrain management systems that allow it to conquer challenging terrain with confidence.

Specifications

  • Engine: The F-150 offers a variety of engines, including a 2.7L EcoBoost V6, 3.5L EcoBoost V6, 5.0L V8, and a hybrid powertrain.
  • Transmission: A 10-speed automatic transmission is standard across the lineup.
  • Payload Capacity: The F-150 can carry up to 3,310 pounds of payload, depending on the configuration.
  • Towing Capacity: The F-150 can tow up to 14,000 pounds, depending on the configuration.
  • Fuel Economy: Varies depending on the engine and trim level, but the F-150 offers competitive fuel economy for a pickup truck.
  • Dimensions: The F-150 is available in various cab and bed configurations to suit different needs.
  • Safety Features: The F-150 comes standard with a range of safety features, including automatic emergency braking, lane departure warning, and blind spot monitoring.
