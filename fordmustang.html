Performance

The Ford Mustang is a true performance powerhouse, delivering a thrilling driving experience that is sure to satisfy even the most discerning enthusiasts. Its sporty acceleration and agile handling make it a joy to drive on any road, while its powerful engines provide an exhilarating burst of speed. Whether you're cruising down the highway or carving corners on a winding road, the Mustang's performance capabilities are sure to impress.

Specifications

  • Engine: The Mustang is available with a 2.3L EcoBoost four-cylinder engine or a 5.0L V8 engine.
  • Transmission: A 10-speed automatic transmission is standard, while a 6-speed manual transmission is available as an option.
  • Horsepower and Torque: The 2.3L EcoBoost engine produces 310 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, while the 5.0L V8 engine produces 460 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque.
  • 0-60MPH Acceleration: The Mustang can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in the mid-four-second range.
  • Fuel Economy: Fuel efficiency varies depending on the engine and transmission, but the Mustang offers competitive fuel economy for a sports car.
  • Dimensions: The Mustang is available in coupe and convertible body styles.
  • Safety Features: The Mustang comes standard with a range of safety features, including automatic emergency braking, lane departure warning, and blind spot monitoring.
