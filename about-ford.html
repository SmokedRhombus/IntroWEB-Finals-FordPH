About Ford Philippines

Learn more about our mission, vision, and values

Our Mission

Ford Philippines aims to provide innovative and reliable vehicles that meet the diverse needs of Filipino customers. The company seeks to contribute to the economic growth of the Philippines by creating jobs and investing in local communities. Additionally, Ford Philippines is committed to promoting sustainable transportation solutions and reducing environmental impact. To enhance customer service and support the local automotive industry, the company establishes strong partnerships with local suppliers and dealerships.

Our Vision

Ford Philippines envisions becoming the leading automotive brand in the Philippines, renowned for its product quality, customer satisfaction, and dedication to sustainability. The company aspires to inspire a passion for driving and adventure among Filipino consumers. Moreover, Ford Philippines seeks to create a positive impact on society through community engagement and corporate social responsibility initiatives. To achieve these goals, the company fosters a culture of innovation and excellence within the organization, attracting and developing top talent.

Our Values

Ford Philippines is guided by a set of core values that shape its culture and decision-making. Customer focus is paramount, ensuring that the customer is always at the center of everything the company does. Integrity is maintained through honest, transparent, and ethical business dealings. Innovation is fostered by continuously seeking new and better ways to do things, embracing technological advancements and staying ahead of the curve. Teamwork is essential, creating a collaborative and supportive work environment where individuals work together to achieve common goals. Diversity and inclusion are valued, respecting the diverse perspectives and backgrounds of all employees to create an inclusive workplace. Sustainability is a priority, striving to minimize environmental impact and promote sustainable practices in all aspects of the business. Finally, excellence is pursued in everything the company does, aiming for the highest standards of quality and performance.

Built Ford Proud

Ford's "Built Ford Proud" campaign is a global marketing initiative that celebrates the brand's heritage, craftsmanship, and commitment to quality. The campaign emphasizes the pride and dedication that goes into building Ford vehicles, highlighting the durability, reliability, and performance that customers can expect. The "Built Ford Proud" slogan is a powerful statement that resonates with consumers, conveying a sense of confidence and trust in the Ford brand. The campaign often features real-life stories of Ford owners who share their experiences and express their pride in owning a Ford vehicle. Through its "Built Ford Proud" campaign, Ford aims to strengthen its connection with customers and build a loyal customer base. The campaign also serves to differentiate Ford from its competitors, positioning the brand as a leader in the automotive industry.
