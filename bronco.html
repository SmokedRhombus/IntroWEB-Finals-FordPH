Ford Bronco

Get Quotation

Performance

The Ford Bronco is a rugged and capable off-road SUV that embodies the spirit of adventure. Its iconic design, reminiscent of the classic Bronco models of the past, combined with modern technology and off-road prowess make it a standout vehicle for those seeking an adventurous driving experience.

Specifications

  • Engine: The Bronco is available with a 2.7L EcoBoost V6 engine or a 3.0L V6 diesel engine.
  • Transmission: A 10-speed automatic transmission is standard.
  • Off-road Features: The Bronco comes standard with four-wheel drive, locking differentials, and terrain management systems.
  • Ground Clearance: The Bronco has a high ground clearance, allowing it to tackle challenging terrain.
  • Payload Capacity: The Bronco can carry a substantial payload, making it a versatile vehicle for work or recreation.
  • Towing Capacity: The Bronco can tow heavy loads, making it a capable choice for towing trailers or boats.
  • Dimensions: The Bronco is a large SUV with ample dimensions for comfortable seating and cargo space.
  • Safety Features: The Bronco comes standard with a range of safety features, including automatic emergency braking, lane departure warning, and blind spot monitoring.
